The Mark

The chest is the brand: a solid container for risk, lit from inside by the very thing you're chasing. The wordmark uses Cinzel — engraved-on-coin, regal but heavy. The descriptor (spin the vault) is set in italic Fraunces to keep the system from feeling like a heraldry exercise.

12 — Voice & Tone

How it Talks

Direct, slightly worn, never patronizing. The bot doesn't celebrate too hard or apologize for losses. It treats the player like an adult who chose to be there.

Voice — DO
  • Use short, weighted lines for outcomes.+2,400g credited. Vault: 14,500g.
  • Acknowledge the loss without softening it.House took it. −500g. Run it back?
  • Speak in the second person, present tense.You're up 8,250g this hour.
  • Lean on the chest / vault / heat metaphor when it fits.The vault's hot. Three jackpots in twenty minutes.
Voice — DON'T
  • No exclamation marks on losses. Ever.You lost! Try again!
  • No emoji-heavy "fun" copy.OOPS 😬 better luck next time! 🍀✨
  • No moral judgement, no nagging.Maybe you should slow down a bit?
  • Don't promise outcomes or hint at "due" wins.You're due for a big one!
